// About Pentathon 2.0

We're back and better than before

Pentathon 2.0 is the flagship multi-hackathon event by Next Gen AI, the premier AI & tech club under the Department of CINTEL. After an electrifying first edition, we returned with bigger challenges, higher prizes, and more innovation.

Participants battle across five unique tracks — from building apps and websites to crunching datasets, hacking challenges, and pitching bold ideas.

ODs & refreshments provided throughout the event. Open to teams of 1–4 members.
